Alevel经济学概念汇总(按字母排序) - 'a'

经济概念

  • Absolute poverty 绝对贫困: Those people who do not have adequate nutritional intake per day, or do not have adequate shelter or clothing in order to survive. The World Bank reports the number of people in countries below a $1.90 a day adjusted for purchasing power parity (PPP).
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 贫困到无法满足日常生活基本需求(吃，穿，住)
    • 相关概念: developing economy, living standards, equity, relative poverty
  • Adjusted Net Saving: Adjusted net saving is a relatively new measure of economic sustainability. It monitors whether
    savings and investment in a country compensate for the depreciation and depletion of physical and
    natural capital and for pollution damages.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 相当于实际有效, 有助于long run发展的saving
    • 相关概念: developing countries
  • Ability to Pay: the condition for effective demand
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 能买得起才能算作demand
    • 相关概念: effective demand
  • Accelerator effect 加速器作用: when economic growth accelerates, investment rises; vise versa
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 经济加速造成investment增加, 减速造成investment减小
    • 相关概念: multiplier (相关但是不同的两个概念), physical investment, induced investment
  • Absolute Advantage 绝对优势: Occurs when a county can produce a product using fewer resources than another nation. If a country using the same factors of production can produce more of a product, then it has an absolute advantage.
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 相同产出使用资源少或相同production input生产效率更高
    • 相关概念: international trade, comparative advantage
  • Ageing population: A change in the age structure of the population within a country, a rising average age and a growing number of people living beyond the standard working ages. Population ageing happens when the median age of a country or region rises due to rising life expectancy and/or declining fertility rates.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 老龄化
    • 相关概念: developing countries, population growth, population structure
  • Aggregate demand 总需求: Total amount of goods and services demanded in the economy at a given time and price level. It is the sum of consumption expenditure, investment expenditure, government expenditure and net exports. (AD=C+I+G+X-M)
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 一个经济的总需求
    • 相关概念: aggregate supply, equilibrium (national) income
  • Aggregate supply shock: Either an inflation shock or a shock to potential national output; adverse aggregate supply shocks of both types reduce output and can increase the rate of inflation.
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 就是突发性影响aggregate supply的时间，比如发生中东战争造成石油价格上涨
    • 相关概念: aggregate supply
  • Advanced/Developed economies: According to the IMF, 35 economies are ‘advanced economies’. 24 in Europe + USA, Canada, Australia, New Zealand, Israel, Japan and South Korea. High real GDP per capita (productivity), better education and health, better infrastructure
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 就是经济已经发展的比较完全(各种设施，服务，法律健全)的国家
    • 相关概念: developing economies, development
  • Adverse selection: Where the expected value of a transaction is known more accurately by the buyer or the seller due to an asymmetry of information; e.g. health insurance. This is also known as the ‘hidden attributes’ problem i.e. someone with a long-established health condition might be able to hide it from a health insurance company.
    • CIE AS, A2
    • 记忆: 就是因为信息不对等，选了不想要的
    • 相关概念: market failure, information failure
  • Adam Smith: One of the founding fathers of modern economics. His most famous work was the Wealth of Nations (1776) - a study of the progress of nations where people act according to their own self-interest - which improves the public good. Smith’s discussion of the advantages of division of labour remains a potent idea.
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 经济学之父 - 亚当斯密
    • 相关概念:
  • Aid Effectiveness: A measure of the quality of aid delivery and maximizing the impact of aid on extreme poverty reduction and sustainable economic development.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 援助的有效性, 比如如果aid都被贪污了就无效了
    • 相关概念: developing countries, trade vs. aid
  • Air Passenger Duty: Air passenger duty is an example of an indirect tax. Air passenger duty is charged on all passenger flights from UK airports. The rate of duty varies according to passenger destination and the class of passenger travel. Band A: 0 - 2,000 miles; Band B: 2,000+ miles
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 就是国际旅行时相当于买了服务, 所以要交消费税
    • 相关概念: international trade, tariff
  • Aggregate supply: Total planned output of goods and services in an economy at a given time and price level
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 一个经济/地区的总供给
    • 相关概念: aggregate demand
  • Altruism 利他主义: The phenomenon in behavioural science for humans to behave with more kindness and fairness than would be the case if they behaved rationally. The is a good example of the principle. Altruism is often linked to the concept of i.e. humans do not like unequal outcomes. Whilst this is usually seen as positive, it can also result in a negative outcome e.g. a person being willing to forego a gain / reward if it means that someone else won’t gain an even better reward.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 看到自己关心的人过得好自己也开心 - 行为经济学的一个重要概念
    • 相关概念: behaviour economics
  • Allocative efficiency: Producing goods and services demanded by consumers at a price that reflect the marginal cost of supply.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 生产了社会/消费者最需要的产品 - 所以没有浪费
    • 相关概念: productive efficiency, Pareto efficiency, dynamic efficiency, social efficiency
  • Anchoring: The use of (usually) irrelevant information as a reference point for helping to make an estimate of an unknown piece of information. In other words, people use an “anchor point” of an event or a value that they know in order to make a decision or estimate. Behavioural scientists describe this as a
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 用简单易理解的目标来帮助做出决定 - 行为经济学的重要概念
    • 相关概念: behavioural economics
  • Average cost: Total cost per unit of output = Total cost / output = TC/Q.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 平均成本=总成本/输出
    • 相关概念: firm theory, marginal cost, total cost, total variable cost, fixed cost, average variable cost
  • Fixed Cost 固定成本: Fixed costs are costs of production which are constant whatever the level of output. Average fixed costs are total fixed costs divided by the number of units of output, that is, fixed cost per unit of output.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 平均固定成本=固定成本/产出数量，固定成本就是企业正常运营的情况下不管产出是多少都不变的成本，比如房租
    • 相关概念: variable cost, total cost
  • Automation 自动化: Developments in robotics and autonomous systems (RAS) are making
    it possible to automate activities that previously could only be done by
    humans, and to assist workers with tasks that cannot be automated. Automation is the use of machines that are substitutes for labour.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 自动化后机器取代人, 生产效率提高
    • 相关概念:
  • Availability Heuristic: Availability is a heuristic whereby people make judgments about the likelihood of an event based on how easily an example, instance, or case comes to mind
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 就是使用简单易用的方法来做决定 - 行为经济学的重要概念
    • 相关概念: behavioural economics
  • Automatic stabilisers 自动稳定器: Automatic fiscal changes as the economy moves through stages of the business cycle – e.g. a fall in tax revenues from the circular flow in a recession.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 一些现有财政政策, 不需要政府主动改变政策就能稳定经济(经济不好的时候自动刺激经济，过热的时候自动冷却经济)
    • 相关概念: discretionary policy, macro policy
  • Austerity 借债: Economic policy aimed at reducing a government’s deficit (or borrowing). Fiscal austerity can be achieved through increases in government revenues - primarily via tax rises - and/or a reduction in government spending or future spending commitments.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 在政府欠债过多的情况下，为了稳定整个经济的信心，政府调整宏观政策来降低budget defict和debts
    • 相关概念: macro policy, contractionary fiscal policy
  • Asymmetric information 信息不对等: This type of market failure exists when one individual or party has much more information than another individual or party, and uses that advantage to exploit the other party. Finance is a market in information – often a potential borrower (such as a small business) has better information on the likelihood that they will be able to repay a loan than the lender.
    • CIE AS, A2
    • 记忆: 信息不对等 - information failure的一种
    • 相关概念: information failure, market failure
  • Association of Southeast Asian Nations (Asean): Association of Southeast Asian Nations – a .
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 东盟 - international integration的一种: free trade area
    • 相关概念: international trade, international integration
  • Asset price bubble 资产价格泡沫: A sustained rise in the prices of financial assets such as housing and equities which takes their values well above long run sustainable levels. Prices can be driven because expectations of future price increases bring new buyers into the market. Aspects of behavioural economics help to explain asset price bubbles.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 经济泡沫，经济过热的时候发生，房价高于使用价值很多，随时有断崖式下降的可能
    • 相关概念: business cycle
  • Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) 亚洲基础设施投资银行: Chinese-led Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB) launched in 2015. It is designed to raise hundreds of billions of dollars to finance railways, ports and other key development projects.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 类似于世界银行的作用，发放低利息贷款帮助发展中国家进行基础建设 - 中国带头建立
    • 相关概念:
  • Asian Development Bank (ADB) 亚洲发展银行: Multilateral development finance institution that promotes the social and economic progress of the Asian and Pacific region.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 类似于世界银行的作用，发放低利息贷款帮助发展中国家进行基础建设
    • 相关概念:
  • Appreciation: A rise in the external value of one currency against another or a basket of currencies. Relates to a floating exchange rate system.
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 货币升值(相对于其他国家货币更值钱)
    • 相关概念: revaluation, depreciation, appreciation
  • Anti-Competitive Behaviour 反竞争行为: Strategies designed to limit the degree of competition inside a market and reinforce the monopoly power of established businesses.
    • CIE A2
    • 记忆: 当企业的market power过大的时候，企业会滥用它的power来获取利益，比如增加价格，限制价格竞争
    • 相关概念: market structure, market power, market failure
  • Anti-dumping tariff 反倾销关税: Anti-dumping import tariffs are allowed under World Trade Organisation rules when cases of dumping have been established.
    • CIE AS
    • 记忆: 反倾销关税
    • 相关概念: protectionism, indirect tax
